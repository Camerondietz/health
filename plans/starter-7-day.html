Your 7-day starter plan.

One week of workouts, meals, hydration targets, and sleep cues — pulled from every pillar of Oakwell. Print it. Stick it on the fridge. Don't aim for perfection; aim for "did most of it."

The 7-day grid

Each day pairs a workout, three simple meals, a hydration target, and a sleep cue.

Mon · Strength A

Workout: Squat 4×6, Bench 4×6, Row 3×10, Plank 3×45s.

Meals: Oats + berries + nuts · Greek salad with grilled chicken · Salmon + roasted potatoes + greens.

Water: 2.5 L. Sleep cue: Phone out of bedroom.

Tue · Zone 2 Cardio

Workout: 40 min easy bike, jog, row, or brisk walk.

Meals: Greek yogurt + chia + fruit · Lentil soup + whole-grain bread · Stir-fry tofu + brown rice + veg.

Water: 3 L. Sleep cue: Sunlight on face within 1 hr of waking.

Wed · Strength B

Workout: Deadlift 3×5, Overhead Press 4×6, Pull-ups 3×AMRAP, Carries 3×40m.

Meals: Veggie omelet + toast · Tuna salad + greens + olive oil · Chicken thighs + quinoa + roasted broccoli.

Water: 2.5 L. Sleep cue: No caffeine after 12pm.

Thu · Mobility + Walk

Workout: 10-min mobility flow + 25-min outdoor walk.

Meals: Smoothie (yogurt + spinach + banana + nut butter) · Black-bean bowl + avocado · Sheet-pan sausage + veg.

Water: 2.5 L. Sleep cue: Hot shower 1 hr before bed.

Fri · Strength C

Workout: Front squat 3×8, Incline DB press 3×10, RDL 3×10, Face pulls 3×15.

Meals: Eggs + sourdough + tomato · Chicken-quinoa bowl + EVOO · Homemade pizza on whole-grain crust + side salad.

Water: 2.5 L. Sleep cue: Dim lights after sundown.

Sat · Intervals

Workout: 10-min warm-up · 4×4 min hard / 3 min easy · cool down.

Meals: Cottage cheese + fruit + seeds · Big Mediterranean salad + sardines · Beef stir-fry + jasmine rice + bok choy.

Water: 3 L + pinch of salt & lemon. Sleep cue: Same wake time as weekdays.

Sun · Long Walk

Workout: 60+ min easy outdoor walk; meal prep after.

Meals: Big breakfast (eggs + greens + sweet potato) · Leftovers · Roast chicken + grain + veg.

Water: 2.5 L. Sleep cue: Lights out by 10:30pm.

Printable grocery list

Produce

  • Spinach, kale, romaine, arugula
  • Broccoli, bok choy, asparagus
  • Bell peppers, tomatoes, cucumbers, carrots
  • Onions, garlic, ginger, lemons, limes
  • Berries, bananas, apples, oranges
  • Avocado, sweet potato, regular potatoes

Protein

  • Chicken thighs, ground turkey or beef
  • Salmon or sardines, canned tuna
  • Eggs, plain Greek yogurt, cottage cheese
  • Tofu or tempeh, lentils, black beans, chickpeas

Whole grains & carbs

  • Rolled oats, quinoa, brown or jasmine rice
  • Whole-grain bread or sourdough
  • Whole-grain pasta or pizza dough

Healthy fats

  • Extra-virgin olive oil, avocado oil
  • Almonds, walnuts, mixed nuts
  • Chia seeds, flax seeds, tahini, nut butter

Pantry & flavor

  • Sea salt, pepper, paprika, cumin, oregano, garlic powder
  • Soy sauce or tamari, vinegar, mustard, hot sauce
  • Canned tomatoes, low-sodium broth

Sunday prep (60 minutes)

  1. Roast a sheet-pan of vegetables (broccoli, peppers, sweet potato).
  2. Cook a big pot of grain (quinoa or rice).
  3. Hard-boil 6 eggs.
  4. Cook 1.5 lb of one protein (chicken thighs or ground turkey).
  5. Wash and tear lettuce; portion into containers.

Now lunch is "open container, assemble" all week.

Track these 5 numbers

  • Steps per day (target: 7,000–10,000)
  • Hours of sleep (target: 7–9)
  • Glasses of water (target: 8–10)
  • Strength sessions per week (target: 2–3)
  • Servings of vegetables per day (target: 4+)
